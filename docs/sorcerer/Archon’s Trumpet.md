**Level**: 7  
**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Target**: _None_  
**Area**: cone-shaped burst  
**Effect**: _None_  
**Duration**: instantaneous  
**Saving Throw**: Fortitude negates (+7 vs. Fortitude negates)  
**Spell Resistance**: Yes (yes)  
**DC**: 17  
**Caster Level**: 1  
**Spontaneous**: Yes

### 📜 Description
