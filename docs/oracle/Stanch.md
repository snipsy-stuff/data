### 📜 Description
You coagulate the target's blood. The target is stabilized if dying, all bleed effects upon it end, and it is immune to bleed effects for the duration of the spell. It also cannot be the source of blood for any spell or effect that requires even a drop of blood be taken from a creature to cast the spell.
