
# Spell: Book Ward
**Class**: Oracle  
**Level**: 2  
**Casting Time**: 1 action  
**Range**: touch  
**Target**: one touched object up to 10 lbs/level  
**Area**:   
**Effect**: _None_  
**Duration**: 1 day/level or until discharged (see text)  
**Saving Throw**: Harmless, Object, Will negates (Will negates (harmless, object))  
**Spell Resistance**: Harmless, Object, Yes (yes (harmless, object))  
**DC**: 12  
**Caster Level**: 1  
**Spontaneous**: Yes

---

### 🔮 Components
- **Components**: Verbal, Somatic, Material or Divine Focus

### 🏫 School
- ** School **: Abjuration
- ** Subschool **: undefined
- ** Descriptor **: undefined
---

### 📜 Description
As protection from energy, except as noted above and that the spell only protects against acid and fire damage. While the energy protection remains, the item is also completely waterproof (this protection extends to other liquids as well, such as alcohol and oil).
